Who is Our project for

Anyone with access to the Internet

Our project is for anyone who just needs to get the gist of a long article. You can read this summary, then go read the original article if you want to.

Students

Our project is for students studying for exams who are overwhelmed by information overload. This tool helps them summarize information into a concise, easy-to-digest text.

Writers

Our project is for anyone who writes frequently and wants to quickly summarize their articles for easier writing, easier reading, better organization, and improved clarity.

Teachers

Our project is for teachers who want to summarize a long document or chapter for their students, making it easier to review, understand, retain, and teach effectively.

Institutions

Our project is for corporations and institutions who want to condense a piece of content into a summary that is easy to digest for their employees/students.

Journalists

Our project is for journalists who need to summarize a long article for their newspaper or magazine, ensuring it is concise, engaging, well-structured, and impactful.
